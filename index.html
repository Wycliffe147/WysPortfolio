About Me

Hi, I'm Wycliffe Mwanganda

I innovate, build & most importantly learn. I love creating useful tools, gadgets & applications. Basically, I like finding solutions to problems.

This website is my space to document what I've made, what I'm learning, and what I'm planning to do next. I plan to explore everything from cybersecurity, programming, video editting, animation, electronics, to building real-world devices based on my ideas.

I'll be sharing videos, lessons learned, and where possible behind-the-scenes looks at how I tackle each project. It's all part of my journey to grow my skills and hopefully inspire others to start their own.

When I'm not building, learning or experimenting, I spend time with my family, but while doing all that it's all spent with God.
